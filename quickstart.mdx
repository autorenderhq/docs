## Create AutoRender Account

Get started with AutoRender by creating your account.

1. Visit the AutoRender website
2. Click on **Sign Up** or **Create Account**
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile setup

Once your account is created, you'll have access to the AutoRender dashboard where you can start uploading and managing your images.
